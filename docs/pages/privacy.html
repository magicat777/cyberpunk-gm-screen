Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Cyberpunk RED GM Screen application.

Information We Collect

The Cyberpunk RED GM Screen is designed to operate entirely in your web browser. It is important to note that:

  • Local Storage Only: All data, including your notes, layout configurations, and preferences, is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.
  • No Personal Information: The application does not collect, process, or store any personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.
  • No Analytics: We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any analytics tools to monitor your usage of the application.
  • No Server Communication: The application operates entirely client-side and does not send or receive data from external servers after the initial page load, except when explicitly using features that require external resources (e.g., linking to external websites).

The Cyberpunk RED GM Screen is a privacy-first application that processes all your data locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use your data for any purposes. The application's features that save data (such as layout saving, notes, and preferences) use your browser's local storage exclusively:

  • Layout Configurations: Your panel layouts are saved in local storage to provide persistence between sessions.
  • Notes Content: Any notes you create are stored in local storage for your convenience.
  • User Preferences: Your settings, such as theme preference and font settings, are stored in local storage to maintain your preferences.

You have complete control over this data and can clear it at any time using your browser's controls for clearing site data or local storage.

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but since all data is stored locally on your device, the primary security responsibility lies with your browser's security features and your device's security measures.

Things to be aware of:

  • Local storage is not encrypted by default in most browsers.
  • Other users with access to your device may be able to access the data stored by this application if they have access to your browser.
  • Clearing your browser cache or local storage will remove all data saved by the application.

External Links

The Cyberpunk RED GM Screen may contain links to external websites, including references to the official Cyberpunk RED resources. These external sites have their own privacy policies, and we have no responsibility or liability for their content or activities.

We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external sites you visit from links within our application.

Feedback and Issue Reporting

If you choose to submit feedback or report issues through our Feedback page, any information you provide is handled as follows:

  • Email addresses or contact information you voluntarily provide will only be used to respond to your inquiry.
  • We do not store this information beyond what is necessary to address your feedback or issue.
  • If you report issues through GitHub, your interactions will be governed by GitHub's privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

The Cyberpunk RED GM Screen is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to remove such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post the updated policy on this page and update the "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
